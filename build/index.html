Tokenomics

The collection has 5 tiers of coffins made of different materials which determine the utility a holder gets - check out rarity and utility section.

All 100% of the tokens are backed by exclusive utility, from online video greetings to dance performances at funerals and nightclubs abroad. All you need is to leave a request with the serial number of your token, and provide transfer and accommodation. Detailed information about the rules of booking is available in Whitepaper.

Deadboys&Deadgirls is an additional PFP collection of five types of rarity.

The only way to get it is to use the utility of the main token and burn it. Thus, it will motivate the community to burn the main tokens and pump the price of the remaining. This is not just an avatar - it confirms that you are part of the Dancing Pallbearers family and contribution to the community.

We are planning a limited whitelist mint on the Magic Eden platform in April 2023 with a mint price of 150 MATIC. Follow the news on our social networks!

Mint

Project's tokenomics aims at constant burning of NFT of all types of rarity to ensure rise in value of the remaining ones.

Each burning of a rare token will be announced in the social media and covered by journalists upon arrival in the country of the owner of the NFT to add value to the collection. All greeting videos from wooden coffins will be published publicly on the social networks of the project (only with your consent)

  • Why is this NFT collection beneficial to all parties?
    The world entertainment tour of the Coffin Dance team and any funerals abroad are possible only with the help of the community, through the NFT lottery. The team raises funds for the tour through crowdfunding, and the lucky ones who mint tokens with such a utility can burn it and earn money in real life. The performance becomes profitable to organize having received it at such a ridiculous price. With each next tour to the clubs and funerals, more and more people will know about tokens, and the less there will be on the market. That makes golden & platinum tokens a valuable asset.
    Tokenomics is designed in such a way that the owner of a platinum token can get a great financial benefit on their own by following a simple instruction for interacting with event agencies and clubs.
    Video greetings and zoom conversations do not require much effort from the team, but give liquidity to all other tokens. If the floor price falls, massive burns will occur, which in turn will reduce the number of core tokens, and lead to the release of additional Deadboys & Deadgirl tokens that will balance the tokenomics.
    Good trading volume is able to earn commission on the secondary market. Benjamin Aidoo is also a holder of a part of the tokens and interested in their growth.
